Virtual Pinball Cabinet

Construction

The cabinet for the virtual pinball machine was constructed out of MDF with basic power tools based off of a quick CAD model in NX 12. The dimensions were modified from a traditional Williams style cabinet to include a 43" 4K TV and a 32" Backglass TV. A 3D printed lockdown bar at the front of the cabinet holds down the playfield acrylic and improves comfort when playing.

Hardware and Software

This cabinet runs off of a gaming pc purchased used running the Pinball FX3 game. The PC wiring was modified slightly to add a powerbutton to the underside of the cabinet as well as two USB ports for a keyboard and mouse.
